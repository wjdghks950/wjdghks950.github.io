About

Ph.D. candidate at BLENDER Lab at University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), currently advised by Professor Heng Ji. I'm also a founding member at Medicas AI, working on drug discovery using multimodal AI.

My research revolves around enhancing the multimodal understanding of the world using Mulimodal Foundation Models (MLLMs, VLMs). My goal is to build next-generation MLLMs that are capable of dealing with: (i) the complex, granular visual information understanding and grounding, (ii) handling the interplay of general and domain-specific knowledge that complement the visual observation, and (iii) bridging the modality gap between image and natural language.

Here are some keywords about my research interests:

  • Multimodal Large Language Models (e.g., Large Vision Language Models)
  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) & Language Modeling
  • Visual Grounding
